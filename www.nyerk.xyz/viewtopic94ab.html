Download "Aion system.cfg editor"

1.) SystemOptionsGraphics.cfg
2.) System.cfg with "Aion system.cfg editor"
r_Width = "XXX"
r_WindowHeight = "XXX"
3.)Enable Write Protection via Right Click > Properties for the files System.cfg and SystemOptionsGraphics.cfg
