Luna weekly S/A rank isnt that easy, monsters are grouped, as soon as you attack one you are pretty much dead, their attack patterns are not predictable. If you wait for NPC to kill all the monsters by their own you will have rank B or lower because it takes 20 minutes or more.

The only way is to aggro monster groups, then go back to spawn zone, wait for NPC to take their aggro, and then help them with AoE skills to clear faster. You also need to DPS the big mechas with the highest dps skill, 1st and 2nd skill, and keep distance to not die by their explosions. Then kill the gunner to spawn the cannon and also be carefull to not get aggro from the side cannons which make huge damage from infinite range, kill the patrol that its right after gate, attack the 1st boss with 3rd skill and keep distance to not being pulled by his first skill, same with last boss.

Honestly there are so many variables to take in account for luna weekly that S rank makes it almost impossible, not to mention the benefits for S rank are (maybe one tempering per week), you can get the quest item even if you get D rank tho .

Anyway I'm down to provide ideas and testing any project cooco is willing to make.
