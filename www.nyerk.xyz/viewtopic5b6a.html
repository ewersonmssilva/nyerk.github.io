New VIP section!

Posted: Mon Feb 27, 2017 6:24 pm
by 0x00
So, we have decided what to do in regards of helping people share their work! We have opened a new group named VIP and a new forum!
This forum can only be accessed if you are a part of the group. So, how to gain access?
It's simple - the more you contribute (LUA source, helping others, bug testing), the more likely it is you are going to be either invited or when you feel you've satisfied the community standards, you may apply!

As an initiative I going to do 2 things:

1. Custom scripts - I am almost 95% familiar with the LUA language and the AionScript framework (how the LUA is interpreted) so any VIP may freely ask to have a custom script made for their class. This custom script will work with both the 0verX framework and the OfficialGrinderFramework.

2. WTFast access (tbc) - I am handing out 2 WTFast ADVANCED subscriptions for now. Eventually I will work this into a sub-nyerkwork so all VIP users can use the GPN service!

3. Teamviewer support (from me - so this is limited to my time and how severe your issue is) - I will tweak / fix / repair any issue you may have!

Ofc the VIP section has many little goodies (scripts) that aren't posted in the normal forums!

So, keep your wallets in your pockets and start using your brains!
