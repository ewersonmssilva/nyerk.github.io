Yeah, it has a few:

Code: Select all

	-- Indicates whether or not to suspend activity with an enemy player in the area.
		ProtectionEnemy = true,
		-- Indicates the action when ProtectionEnemy is true and an enemy is found. Console command, i.e. "/Skill Return" or "/Use Morheim Fortress Scroll".
		ProtectionEnemyEscape = "/Skill Return",
		-- Indicates whether or not to suspend activity with a friendly player in the area.
		ProtectionFriendly = true,
Also cooco posted a protection script to logout if certain player is in your range

viewtopic.php?f=8&t=126

I believe with a few modifications it can make your character logout if anyone is in range despite their nickname.

Regarding your ban, its hard to tell if you were checked by a GM or reported by lots of players, the best way is to use the protection methods mentioned before, its better to reconnect every now and then than loosing your account.
